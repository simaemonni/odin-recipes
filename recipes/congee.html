Congee

I will be removing my wisdom teeth in a couple days and heard that you should avoid solid foods for a while. In preparation, I will be sharing the most basic congee recipe with everyone!

Congee is sort of like rice soup. Some like theirs a little more watery and some prefer a thicker viscosity. It is also an incredibly accommodating dish--add whatever kind of meat, vegetable and seasoning you'd like.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 10-12 cups water or stock

Steps

  1. Wash the rice in advance and then soak with clean water for 30 minutes. Mix with 1/2 tablespoon of vegetable cooking oil or sesame oil. Set aside for 15 minutes. You can freeze the rice in the previous day after soaking to reduce the cooking time.
  2. Bring the water to a boiling in a pot and then add the soaked rice. Heat until boiling again and slow down the fire and continue cooking for 30 minutes.
  3. If you want the rice to be well broken, stir the porridge during the cooking in one direction in the last 5 minutes.
  4. Serve with your favorite sides and garnishes.
  5. Enjoy!
